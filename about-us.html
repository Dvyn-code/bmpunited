Who We Are

Basil, Mary and Peter (bmp) United is a nonprofit organization founded to honor God, Basil, Mary, and Peter, parents, and mentor of the founder. bmp unites donors, who provide the resources, associates who do the work, and beneficiaries who benefit from bmpUnited mission.

Who Does The Work?

bmpUnited Associates or Team Members are volunteers that donate their time and expertise to plan and execute bmpUnited projects. Depending on their specific roles, an associate may receive a small allowance or be reimbursed for expenses incurred on behalf of bmpUnited. You are welcome to join and make a difference in the lives of other people. Reach us though the Contact page.
