The administrator of bmpUnited worked in Information Technology (IT) for over forty years, perhaps longer than his life-time role model Bill Gates. Just like Bill Gates, the industry was kind and user friendly to this administrator, although not to the same magnitude as Bill Gates. What makes a difference is what one does with what they have.

bmpUnited was created amidst the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, to give needed services to others, and show gratitude for a fun IT career. The complete details are published in an autobiography: Young Man In A Hurry.
