B asil, Mary and Peter (bmp) United is a nonprofit organization founded to honor God, Basil, Mary, and Peter, parents, and mentor of the founder. bmp unites donors, who provide the resources, associates who do the work, and beneficiaries who benefit from bmpUnited mission.

When donors and associates succeed in lifting beneficiaries up, society wins. Please read about the benefits of helping people, especially when, and where, the help is actually needed. Our type of help must target root causes to avoid dependency.
