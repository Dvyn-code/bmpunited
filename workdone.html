Accomplishments

Years of the pandemic, 2020, 2021 and 2022 were the busiest years for bmpUnited. With so many people, all over the world, needing help, the pandemic gave birth to bmpUnited. Prior to the pandemic, bmpUnited was a mere concept on the drawing board.

Below are bmpUnited footprints established in three years of service. Working mostly with volunteers and one single source of funding, we accomplished so much.

In 2023, we looked back to examine lessons learned in those three years. While carefully managing the existing projects, bmpUnited is rapidly implementing changes from lessons learned to keep bmpUnited alive.
