Who Is Wawasa?

WAWASA or Wawa Solar Ambassadors Enterprises is a registered (CRBN1137976) solar installation firm that specializes in solar energy education, marketing, installation and maintenance services for the benefit of the community in which it resides.

WAWASA’s mission is to provide renewable energy sources as a competitive supplement to power provided to the community from other sources, such as Enugu Electric Distribution Company (EEDC) and portable gas generators.

WAWASA is dedicated to replacing community blackouts with reliable electricity supply, only limited by the customer's ability to afford the services.

By focusing on its mission, WAWASA provides an alternative to the blackout experienced by EEDC customers and the pollution and noise produced by most portable generators. WAWASA is an independent company used by bmpUnited for solar projects.

Whenever feasible, bmpUnited, WAWASA and customers within the community enter into tripartite loan agreements to make solar systems more affordable.
